Sourdough Biscotti

- 1/4 c olive oil
- 1 1/3 c brown sugar, packed
- 2 eggs
- 1 t vanilla extract
- 1 T almond extract
- 1/2 c sourdough discard
- 2 1/2 c all-purpose flour
- 3/4 t unrefined sea salt
- 1 c raw whole almonds
Optional chocolate dip:
- 8 oz semi sweet baker's chocolate*
- 1/4 tsp coconut oil
1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees and arrange raw almonds on a sheet tray prepared with parchment.
2. Once the oven is preheated, toast the almonds for 8-12 minutes. Set the almonds aside to cool slightly and then coarsely chop them.
3. Meanwhile, in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment, mix together olive oil and brown sugar. Add eggs one at a time and mix well. Scrape down the sides as you go.
4. Next, add vanilla extract, almond extract and sourdough discard. Mix again. Add the flour and salt and mix for a few rotations until just combined.
5. Pour in the coarsely chopped almonds and give it another few turns to mix. Take care not to over mix the dough.
6. On the same (cooled) sheet tray you previously used, form the dough into 2 equal sized logs approximately 3 1/2 to 4 inches wide. Once the logs are formed, chill the dough for 30 minutes.
7. Increase the oven temperature to 350 and bake the logs for 30 minutes. Rotate the sheet tray at the 15 minute mark to achieve an even bake.
8. Remove the sheet tray from the oven and allow the logs to cool for 15-20 minutes, then use a serrated knife to carefully cut thin slices of biscotti 1/2" to 3/4" thick.
9. Arrange the slices on a baking sheet and place back in the oven for 15-25 minutes (depending on how thin/thick you've sliced them). Flip them halfway through baking and give the middle a tap to see if it's crisping up.
10. Once they are crispy and baked in the center, remove from the oven and allow them to fully cool.
11. Melt chocolate over a double boiler and dip the biscotti, scraping of the excess chocolate, and then set on parchment for the chocolate to cool and set.
*optional substitute: 3 T unsweetened cocoa powder + 1 T vegetable oil, canola oil, or butter (one of the former two if df)
Ingredients and recipe from: Bass, Lisa. “Sourdough Biscotti.” Farmhouse on Boone, 28 June 2022, www.farmhouseonboone.com/sourdough-biscotti

Apple Cinnamon Muffins

- 1/2 c packed (light) brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 1/2 t vanilla extract
- 1 large, firm apple (I prefer fuji or gala but other types work, too) -- peeled (optional, I prefer not peeled) and cut into chunks according to preferred size
- 1 c all-purpose flour
- 3/4 t baking powder
- 3/4 t cinnamon
- 1/4 t salt
- 6 T unsalted butter, melted and cooled*
1. Preheat to 350
2. Whisk brown sugar, egg, and vanilla in large bowl until smooth
3. Stir in apple, flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt until almost combined
4. Stir in butter until fully combined; pour into muffin tin which has been greased or which has cupcake liners in it
5. Bake 20-22 minutes or until fork/toothpick comes out almost clean
*dairy free butter or 6 tablespoons (equal to 3/8 cups) of applesauce if df
Ingredients and recipe from: “Apple Cinnamon Muffins.” The Hungry Hutch, 6 Dec 2020, www.thehungryhutch.com/apple-cinnamon-muffins-recipe/

Nestlé Toll House Chocolate Chip Cookies

- 2 1/4 c all-purpose flour
- 1 t baking soda
- 1 t salt
- 1 c (16 T) butter, softened*
- 3/4 c granulated sugar
- 3/4 c packed brown sugar
- 1 t vanilla extract
- 2 eggs
- 2 c chocolate chips**
- 1 c chopped nuts (0ptional -- if omitting, add 1 to 2 T all-purpose flour)
1. Preheat to 375
2. Mix flour, baking soda and salt in small bowl
3. Beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar and vanilla extract in large mixing bowl until creamy
4. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition
5. Gradually beat in flour mixture
6. Stir in morsels and nuts
7. Obtain via rounded tablespoon or cookie scooper and drop onto baking sheets with parchment paper
8. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until golden brown
9. Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes, then move to wire racks to cool completely
*dairy free butter or 3/4 cups applesauce if df
**dairy free chocolate or dark chocolate if df
Ingredients and recipe from: “Original NESTLÉ® TOLL HOUSE® Chocolate Chip Cookies.” NESTLÉ® TOLL HOUSE®, verybestbaking.com, www.verybestbaking.com/toll-house/recipes/original-nestle-toll-house-chocolate-chip-cookies/
