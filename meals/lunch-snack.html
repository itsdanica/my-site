Sourdough Crackers

- 1 c sourdough discard
- 3/4 c all-purpose flour
- 4 T butter*
- 1 T dried herbs (basil, oregano, parsley, rosemary, thyme are all good options, or a combination of any of those)
- 1 t salt 1. Preheat to 350
2. Mix all of the ingredients together
3. Knead the dough just until it comes together
4. Place the dough between two pieces of parchment paper and roll it out as thin as possible
5. Place the rolled out cracker dough and parchment paper onto a baking sheet
6. Bake for 15 minutes or until crispy
7. Cut with knife of break apart and store in an air-tight container
*dairy free butter, or 3 T (equal to 3/16 cups) applesauce
Recipe and ingredients from: Bass, Lisa. “Sourdough Discard Crackers.” Farmhouse on Boone, 9 Sept 2022, www.farmhouseonboone.com/sourdough-crackers

Chickpea Salad

- 1 standard size (about 14-16 oz) can chickpeas -- drained, rinsed, and mashed with a fork
- 1/2 c finely chopped pickles (optional -- blech!)
- 1/2 c finely chopped celery stalk
- 1/4 c finely chopped onions
- 1/4 c mayo
- Pickle juice to taste (optional :P)
- Dash of mustard
1. Mix well, adjust if necessary
Ingredients and recipe from: my mother
